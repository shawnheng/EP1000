Project Title: Mearm

What does it do?

Ideation

My project idea was to create a MEARM (robot arm). Which allow it to pick and place objects and rotate around.

For MEARM, I use a template from thingiverse to cut robot arm parts and later assemble them with M3 screws.

MEARM File

Once I download the template. I proceed on to laser cutter. For the MEARM, what materials to use for laser cutting. you can choose either plywood or acrylic. For me, I am going to cutter out using plywood.

Once I cut all the template of MEARM parts, I proceed to assemble everything.

MEARM assembly

I use this website to follow step by step on assembling the parts together.
